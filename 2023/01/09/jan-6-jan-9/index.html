“Seiron”

産経新聞出版局, サンケイ出版, and 産業経済新聞社. 1973. 正論 産経新聞出版局. https://ci.nii.ac.jp/ncid/AN00370131.

  • 2022 Feb, no articles mentioning this topic
  • 2022 Mar, some articles touched on the concept of “family,” which seems deeply affected by doctrines of the “Unification Church” yet did not mention the term “Unification Church.”
  • no mention of “Unification Church” until 2022 Oct

    • 阿比留瑠比. 「空気」が支配する日本社会の異常. 産経新聞社, 2022.

    • 八木秀次. 「反アベ」の狂気ここに極まれり. 産経新聞社, 2022.
    • One criticized both right and left wings for this problem while partly praising the “Unification Church” related organizations’ works, primarily related to N. Korea’s kidnapping of the Japanese.
    • 三浦小太郎. 旧統一教会問題の本質は何か. 産経新聞社, 2022.

After Oct 2022, they often discussed about this issue. On Jan 2023, they featured the “Unification Church” related issues.
